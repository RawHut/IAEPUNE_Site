Company Overview

IAE Pune is a Pune‑based education and career consultancy dedicated to helping Indian students and professionals access Germany’s vocational training (Ausbildung) programs and broader study‑abroad or job opportunities.

Here’s the key information about our offerings and approach:

Core Expertise

  • Ausbildung Support: End‑to‑end guidance into Germany’s dual‑system vocational training—combining classroom theory with paid on‑the‑job practice.
  • Study‑Abroad & Job Consulting: Assistance with university applications, CV preparation, interview coaching, and placement with German employers.

Comprehensive Services

  • Career Counseling: One‑on‑one sessions to assess goals, match fields (IT, healthcare, mechatronics, logistics, tourism, nursing, etc.), and plan pathways.
  • German Language Coaching: Structured courses up to B2 level; preparation for Goethe exams A1–B2.
  • Documentation & Visa Assistance: Help with preparing CVs, cover letters, certificates, visa applications, embassy interviews, and travel logistics.
  • Post‑Arrival Support: Airport pickup, accommodation search, local registrations (police, town hall), bank setup, translation/notarization, residence‑permit extensions, and ongoing in‑country assistance.

Value Proposition

  • Earn While You Learn: Apprenticeship stipends of €1,000–€1,500/month during Ausbildung.
  • High Placement Rates: Partnerships with reputable German companies and institutions to maximize job‑offer likelihood.
  • Transparent & Affordable: Direct, intermediary‑free process to cut costs and ensure honest guidance.
  • Hands‑On Mentorship: Personalized support from initial consultation through to settling in Germany.

Extended Network & Community

  • Alumni Network: A growing pool of graduates for mentorship, professional networking, and social events in Europe and India.
  • Local Expertise: Coordinators like Mr Vishal B and Niyath Bansode, plus a team experienced in both Indian and German education systems.
