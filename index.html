IAE Pune

Education Abroad & Careers in Germany

Helping Indian students build careers in Europe through Ausbildung

What We Do

We help students from India apply for Germany's Ausbildung programs, offering full support including career counseling, German language training, application & visa guidance, and post-arrival services.

Who We Are

IAE Pune is a trusted education consultancy with a focus on helping students begin successful vocational careers in Germany. We are passionate about guiding youth to international opportunities.

What is Ausbildung?

Ausbildung is Germany's vocational training program combining theoretical education and on-the-job practice. Lasting between 2 to 3.5 years, it's open to German and international students, including Indians.
