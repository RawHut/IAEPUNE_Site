What is Ausbildung?

Ausbildung is Germany's vocational training program combining theoretical education and on-the-job practice. Lasting between 2 to 3.5 years, it's open to German and international students, including Indians.

Benefits for Indian Students

  • Earn while you learn, reducing financial burden.
  • Gain internationally recognized skills and practical experience.
  • High demand for skilled workers in Germany.
  • Pathway to permanent employment and settlement.
  • Work with leading global companies.

Types of Ausbildung

Dual System

Combines classroom instruction with hands-on work at a partner company.

School-Based

Predominantly classroom-focused with practical training modules.

Popular Fields

  • IT & Engineering
  • Healthcare & Nursing
  • Mechatronics & Logistics
  • Automotive & Tourism
  • Physiotherapy & Medical Imaging
  • Surgical & Anesthesia Assistance

Requirements for Indian Students

  • Minimum education: 12th grade or equivalent.
  • German proficiency: B1-B2 level.
  • Application via German companies, job portals, or German Employment Agency.
  • Some programs may require entrance exams or prior experience.
  • Visa application process for Ausbildung in Germany.

How to Apply from India

  1. Research suitable Ausbildung programs.
  2. IAE assists in choosing high-paying fields aligned with your interests.
  3. Enroll in German language courses through IAE.
  4. IAE prepares your documents (CV, cover letter, certificates).
  5. IAE provides interview training and assessments.
  6. IAE applies for your visa and coordinates travel to Germany.

Salary & Future Prospects

Monthly stipend ranges from €1,000 to €1,500, increasing annually during training. High likelihood of permanent employment, opportunities for further study or specialization, and possibility to bring family upon securing a job.
